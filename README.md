# Statics-Optimized-Bench
Bench redesigned to improve static equilibrium by lowering center of gravity and increasing critical tilt angle through structural optimization and team collaboration.

For this project, I collaborated with classmates to design a structurally superior bench compared to the original model. We improved static stability by lowering the center of gravity and replacing rigid wood seating with tensioned rope elements to enhance equilibrium performance

My primary role was leading the complete bench design in Onshape, collaborating closely with the team assembler, and finalizing the technical drawings for fabrication.

Overall, the project was a success. The final design ranked #1 in stability performance within the statics course and achieved the lowest overall weight among all competing designs!
